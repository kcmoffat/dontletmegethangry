Friends don't let friends get hangry

Warn the people you love now before hanger sets in.

You get hangry. We get it.

It's 5pm (hell even 10am sometimes) and you're wondering what you're going to eat for dinner. You get nervous, stressed - sometimes even silent, depending on your particular strain of hanger. Things spiral out of control quickly. Your body shuts down, ironically too tired to do the one thing that could cure hanger.

There's a better way.

There is no cure for hanger (other than eating some god damned food), but there's a new way to prevent it using innovative technology. You have someone you entrust with your hanger prevention, whether it be your significant other, friend, roommate, parent, frenemy, or other chosen one. Why not warn them before it's too late?

Enter the Hangry Warning.

When you first feel the pangs of hunger, send a hangry warning. The recipient will immediately get an email about your impending hanger, so they can get off their ass and take care of food time. The warning will be accompanied by a random tasteful picture like the one to the right. It's that simple. Hanger averted.
